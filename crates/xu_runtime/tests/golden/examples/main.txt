========================================
       Bank System Demo
========================================

Features demonstrated:
  - Structs with static and instance methods
  - Enums with data (TransactionResult, TransferResult)
  - Match expressions for pattern matching
  - Collections (dict, list)

--- Setup: Creating Customers and Accounts ---
Registered: Customer #1: Alice Smith <alice@example.com> [Standard]
Registered: Customer #2: Bob Jones <bob@example.com> [Standard]
Opened: Account #1000 (Checking) - Alice Smith: $1000 
Opened: Account #1001 (Savings) - Alice Smith: $5000 
Opened: Account #1002 (Checking) - Bob Jones: $2500 

--- Account Operations Test ---
Testing account: Account #1000 (Checking) - Alice Smith: $1000 
Deposit $500: Success! New balance: $1500
Withdraw $200: Success! New balance: $1300
Withdraw $10000: Failed: Insufficient funds ($1300 available)
Deposit -$100: Failed: Invalid amount
Final state: Account #1000 (Checking) - Alice Smith: $1300 

--- Freeze Operations Test ---
Account before freeze: Account #1001 (Savings) - Alice Smith: $5000 
Account after freeze: Account #1001 (Savings) - Alice Smith: $5000 [FROZEN]
Deposit while frozen: Failed: Account is frozen
Deposit after unfreeze: Success! New balance: $5100

--- Transfer Operations Test ---
Before transfer:
  Account #1000 (Checking) - Alice Smith: $1300 
  Account #1002 (Checking) - Bob Jones: $2500 
Transfer $300 from 1000 to 1002: Transfer complete! From: $1000, To: $2800
Transfer $50000 from 1000 to 1002: Source error: Insufficient funds: $1000 available
After transfers:
  Account #1000 (Checking) - Alice Smith: $1000 
  Account #1002 (Checking) - Bob Jones: $2800 

--- Customer Tier Test ---
Customer: Customer #1: Alice Smith <alice@example.com> [Standard]
Discount rate: 0%
After upgrade: Customer #1: Alice Smith <alice@example.com> [Silver]
New discount rate: 5%
After 2 more upgrades: Customer #1: Alice Smith <alice@example.com> [Platinum]
Final discount rate: 15%

--- Total Balance Test ---
Alice's total balance: $6100
Bob's total balance: $2800

--- Final State ---
=== All Customers ===
  Customer #2: Bob Jones <bob@example.com> [Standard]
  Customer #1: Alice Smith <alice@example.com> [Platinum]

=== All Accounts ===
  Account #1002 (Checking) - Bob Jones: $2800 
  Account #1000 (Checking) - Alice Smith: $1000 
  Account #1001 (Savings) - Alice Smith: $5100

       Demo Complete
